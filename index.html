Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our grooming and walking services.

Grooming Services

How long does a grooming session take?

Grooming sessions typically take 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on your dog's breed, size, coat condition, and the services requested. Puppies and first-time clients may require a little extra time to ensure they feel comfortable.

What if my dog has sensitive skin or allergies?

Please inform us in advance. We can use hypoallergenic or medicated shampoos if needed. If your dog has a medical condition, we recommend consulting your vet before booking.

Do you groom puppies?

Yes! We offer puppy introduction grooming sessions to gently familiarize young dogs with the grooming process. Early exposure helps build positive grooming habits.

What happens if my dog has matted fur?

Matted fur can be uncomfortable and sometimes painful for dogs. If severe matting is present, we may recommend a safe shave-down to prevent injury. We will always inform you before proceeding.

Dog Walking Services

How long is each dog walking session?

Our standard walks are 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on your dog's needs. We tailor the pace and duration based on your dog's age, energy level, and health condition.

Are the walks private or group walks?

We primarily offer private one-on-one walks to ensure personalized attention and safety. Small group walks may be available for compatible dogs upon request.

Is my dog safe during walks?

Yes. Your dog's safety is our top priority. We use secure leashes and harnesses, avoid unsafe or overcrowded areas, monitor weather conditions, and stay alert at all times. We also follow your specific instructions regarding routes and preferences.

Do you walk puppies or senior dogs?

Absolutely. We adjust the pace and duration to suit puppies (shorter, gentler walks) and senior dogs (slow-paced, comfort-focused walks).

Additional Information

What vaccinations are required?

For the safety of all pets, dogs should be up-to-date on core vaccinations before their appointment.

How should I prepare my dog for their appointment?

Ensure your dog has had a bathroom break and avoid feeding a large meal before the appointment.

Can I stay during my dog's grooming session?

To ensure a calm environment, we recommend allowing us to work one-on-one with your pet. However, we're happy to provide updates during the session if requested.

Do you offer emergency or same-day bookings?

Same-day appointments may be available depending on our schedule. Please contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp for urgent requests.
